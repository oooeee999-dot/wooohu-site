The Heritage of WOOOHU

Established in 2016 and situated in the core of Jieyang's premier footwear industry zone, WOOOHU (Gaomeilun) has dedicated a decade to the art of shoemaking. We specialize exclusively in stylish women's sandals and fashion slides, blending the soul of East Asian minimalist design with rigorous industrial standards.

Over the past 10 years, our focus has remained steadfast: creating footwear that speaks of "Quiet Luxury." From the selection of premium textures to the ergonomic engineering of every sole, each pair of mules and sandals is a testament to our craftsmanship. As a leading sandals manufacturer, we offer robust wholesale and bespoke manufacturing services (OEM/ODM), empowering global boutique brands and distributors with a unique aesthetic language and reliable Chinese manufacturing excellence.
